Pizza

Pizza is an Italian dish featuring a flat, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like pepperoni, vegetables, or mushrooms. Baked until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted, pizza comes in many styles, including thin crust, deep dish, and wood-fired, making it a beloved choice globally.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup fat-free milk
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

No-Yeast Pizza Dough Pre Steps

You’ll need just five kitchen staple ingredients to make this no-yeast pizza dough recipe:

  • Flour: This yeastless pizza crust starts with all-purpose flour.
  • Baking powder: Without yeast, the pizza dough needs baking powder to rise.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the flavor and strengthens the gluten.
  • Milk: You’ll need ½ cup of fat-free milk.
  • Olive oil: Olive oil locks in moisture and keeps the dough from drying out.

Steps to Make No-Yeast Pizza Dough

  1. Mix the dry ingredients, then stir in the wet ingredients until a dough forms.
  2. Turn out the dough and knead.
  3. Shape the dough into a ball and let rise.
  4. Roll the dough out into a circle.
  5. Add your favorite toppings and bake. Enjoy!
